Mozilla Contacts

Mozilla Contacts helps you manage your online life by bringing together all of your address books and friend lists in one place.

Once you have set up your Mozilla Contacts, web applications that use the Open Web Applications can use your contacts with no additional setup required. (You will always be in control of access to your data, of course!)

Mozilla will never sell your address lists to anyone or use them to contact your friends without your permission.

To get started, click on one of these services to link it to Mozilla Contacts.
